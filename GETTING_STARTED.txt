┃ 📝 DATA FORMAT                                                              ┃
┗━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━┛

  Your CSV file must have these exact columns:

  date          date in YYYY-MM-DD format
  category      Text (Electronics, Furniture, Others, etc.)
  region        Text (Maharashtra, Karnataka, etc.)
  revenue       Number (no commas or currency symbols)
  quantity      Number (integer)
  cost          Number

  Example:
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  date,category,region,revenue,quantity,cost
  2024-01-15,Electronics,Maharashtra,50000,10,25000
  2024-01-16,Furniture,Karnataka,30000,5,15000
  2024-01-17,Others,Tamil Nadu,20000,8,10000
